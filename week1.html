    PROYECT: "INTELLIGENT EXTERNAL FIXATOR"

    My project for the FAB Academy 2019, is an integilente external fixator. This will help the recovery of complex fractures; in a quick way and with fewer sequels. In addition, this device will be controlled both by the patient and mainly by the doctor by smartphone..

    READ MORE

    COMPLEX FRACTURES

    Complex fractures are difficult to manage. Because these fractures damage and even damage the bone, as well as the tissues around the muscle. The use of plates and screws is often impossible. The external fixator is used in this type of fracture.

    Get Started

    CONVENTIONAL EXTERNAL FIXATOR

    The conventional external fixator is a simple and widely used system. But the handling becomes very difficult, because control is performed in a single, axial plane. Often presenting deformations when the fracture has healed.

    COMPLEX EXTERNAL FIXATOR

    The complex external fixator is a complex and very little used system. This system has very good results; but the quantity of pieces of the device makes it very little used.
